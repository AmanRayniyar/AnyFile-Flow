Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about AnyFile Flow

Are the tools really free to use? +

Yes, all tools on AnyFile Flow are completely free to use. There are no hidden costs, subscription fees, or limitations on usage. We believe in providing accessible tools for everyone.

How do you ensure my files remain private? +

All file processing happens directly in your browser using client-side technologies. Your files never leave your device or get uploaded to our servers, ensuring complete privacy and security.

What file formats do you support? +

We support a wide range of file formats including PDF, DOC, DOCX, PPT, PPTX, XLS, XLSX, JPG, PNG, GIF, WEBP, MP4, AVI, MOV, MP3, WAV, and many more. Our tools are regularly updated to support new formats.

Can I use these tools on mobile devices? +

Absolutely! AnyFile Flow is fully responsive and works perfectly on all devices including smartphones and tablets. The interface adapts to your screen size for optimal usability.
